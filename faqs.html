FAQs

Getting started

How quickly can we start a project?
+
Most projects start within 1-2 weeks after discovery. We begin with a kickoff workshop to align goals, scope, and delivery milestones.
Do you work with startups and enterprise teams?
+
Yes. We support early-stage teams launching MVPs and larger organizations modernizing core platforms.

Pricing & timelines

How do you price your services?
+
We offer fixed-scope pricing for clearly defined projects and monthly retainers for iterative product development.
How long does a typical project take?
+
Small projects can ship in 4-8 weeks. Larger platforms generally run 3-6 months depending on complexity.

Technology

Which technologies do you use?
+
We build with modern web stacks including React, Node.js, TypeScript, Python, cloud platforms, and API-first architectures.
Can you work with our existing codebase?
+
Absolutely. We can audit your current system, improve maintainability, and extend features without disrupting operations.

Support
